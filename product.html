WHAT IS COATVID-19?

Coatvid 19 is a coat like clothing made out of environment safe mask like features. It has 5 filters to protect you from spreading diseases and getting diseases from public.

We plan to make a suit that can help us prevent getting contagious diseases.

It also has daily surveys to keep track of your health. When you first receive our product, it comes with a free swab test operated by our staff.

You can choose not to plant the coat but wherever it goes, it will still decompose and benefit the environment.

FEATURES

Coatvid-19 is a washable stainproof material that includes not one, not two, but FIVE filters. Having the third filter bea waterpoof, Coatvid-19 ensures that your sneeze and coughs wont be going anywhere.

Our product is goverment approved and funded, meaning Coatvid-19 is 100% FREE, however you may help us manufacture more by donating through our app!

The three layers of a normal mask are polypropylene filters, hygroscopic fabric and non woven fabric. In any of those layers, you can find a seed to plant when it’s worn out.

NEW FEATURES

Yours is wearing out? No worries! Simply request for one in our survey.

Seems like such a waste to cut open and throw out right? Well our team improved on the design making it able to grow plants through the seeds inside the layers of edible plastic, which makes it more breathable because of the recycled materials

How do you open the suit and remove it? There is a zipper feature on the neck part, open it and wear the coat feet first and head last then slowly close the zipper again. This helps us to also remove the head part but keep the body suit while eating.

The 2 new filters are the plant leaves made filter so that you can breathe properly and recycle the coat. The other filter is a stretchable garter like material to fit all sizes. There is one size for all plus sized people and one size for all normal sized and thin people. It can be designed by the people because markers or paint won’t cause damage to this as long as it’s cleaned after designing.
