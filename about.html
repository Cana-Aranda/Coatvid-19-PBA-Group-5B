WHO ARE WE?

We are group 5AB, grade 8 students from APEC SCHOOLS that are planning to bring back the normal during this pandemic. Our product’s goal is to help decrease the spread of contagious diseases most especially Covid -19 but also ensure comfort and environment safe materials. The app and plant seeds included with our product are only optional. We hope that our product can help save human lives and plant lives during this pandemic.
