BLACK PLAGUE AND COVID 19

The black plague was a depressing pandemic that caused damage to both the medical industry and economic growth of the countries that were affected by it. They weren’t prepared and there wasn’t a fast and specific cure for that so many people lost loved ones before.

Just like the black plague, this years current pandemic started in December 2019 in Wuhan, China and caused a massive break out of Coronavirus. It is believed to come from bats and has no specific cure at the moment. Many people have died and were affected by the Coronavirus and a lot also got healed but it’s still a very risky ailment. With quarantine coming a long we’ve decided to make Coatvid-19 to help bring back the normal face to face life during this pandemic but still stay safe from any contagious diseases most especially, from Covid - 19.
